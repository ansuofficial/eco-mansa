About

ECOMANSA is committed to creating a vibrant academic community by representing, guiding, and serving students, fostering an environment that enriches educational experiences and advances individual interests. Through proactive initiatives and inclusivity, we strive to address academic needs, enhance personal growth, and contribute to the overall well-being of our diverse student body. Our mission is to empower students with the resources and support they need to thrive academically and personally, fostering a strong sense of belonging and community within the University of The Gambia's School of Business and Public Administration.

ECOMANSA envisions a future where knowledge, leadership, innovation, and patriotism are the cornerstones of student development. We aspire to inspire students to become lifelong learners, encouraging critical thinking and compassionate leadership. Our vision extends to nurturing a sense of responsibility and civic duty, with the ultimate goal of empowering students to make meaningful contributions to their communities and play a vital role in national development.
